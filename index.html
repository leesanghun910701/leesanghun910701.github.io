Biography

Sang Hun, Lee received the B.S. and M.S. degree in Nuclear and Quantum engineering from Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), Daejeon, Republic of Korea, in 2014, and 2016. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in Mechanical, Aerospace, and Nuclear engineering at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI), Troy, NY. His research interest includes the probabilistic risk assessment of digitalized nuclear power plant and nuclear safety-critical software reliability assessment.
